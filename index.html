How it works

From the counter to the rack in five steps.

1

Snap

Staff photograph the front, back, and tag of an item right at the buy counter — on a phone or tablet.

2

Analyze

The AI reads the photos and drafts a structured intake card: brand, category, size, color, condition, and possible flaws.

3

Review

Staff check the fields against your buying and pricing rules, adjusting anything that needs a human eye.

4

Approve

A manager confirms the buy / pass / consign call and the final price. This step is required — the AI never decides alone.

5

Export

Approved items become POS-ready titles and descriptions, exported to your point of sale as a clean CSV.
