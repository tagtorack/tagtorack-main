How it works

From your link to your rack in five steps.

1

Share your link

Drop your branded seller link and QR code on Instagram, your shop window, or your receipts — your "scan to sell" sign.

2

Sellers submit

Locals photograph the front, back, and tag of items they want your store to take — right from their phone.

3

AI pre-screens

The AI checks every item against the criteria you set — accepted brands, quality, seasonality, category, and price. Only the matches reach your queue.

4

You select what fits

Approve or pass on each piece from your phone, and a manager confirms the call. This step is required — the AI never decides alone.

5

Seller drops off

Approved sellers are automatically notified to book a drop-off time — no phone tag. Approved items export POS-ready when you want them.
