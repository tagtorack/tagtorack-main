Before you book

The questions store owners ask first.

Does the AI decide what my store buys?

No. The AI screens submissions and drafts an intake card — brand, condition, a suggested price band, a POS-ready listing. A manager approves, adjusts, or passes on every item. Nothing is bought, priced, or listed until a person says so.

What stops me from drowning in junk submissions?

You set the rules — accepted brands, categories, quality, seasonality, and price. Items that miss them never reach your queue. You only ever look at pieces that already fit your store.

How long does setup take?

About a week. We configure your buying rules, brand list, and pricing rubric, generate your branded seller link and QR code, then run a hands-on pilot with your team before you go live.

Do I have to change my point-of-sale system?

No. Approved items export POS-ready as a tidy CSV, alongside weekly inventory and pricing summaries. Tag to Rack sits in front of whatever you already use.

Is there a long contract?

No. You start with a pilot. If the stream of pre-screened inventory isn't earning its place at your counter, you walk away.

Whose data is it?

Yours. Your buying rules, your submissions, and your approval history stay with your store, with a full audit trail of who approved what and when.
