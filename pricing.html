Questions

Pricing & pilot FAQ

Does the AI make final buying decisions?

No. Tag to Rack only prepares recommendations. A manager approves every purchase, price, and listing before anything moves — that approval step is required and can't be turned off.

Why does it cost more than my POS software?

Tag to Rack isn't a point of sale — it's an AI layer that runs on top of the POS you already use. It's priced against the staff hours it saves at the buy counter, not against POS subscriptions. Most stores find it costs less than adding part-time intake help, while making pricing more consistent.

What does the setup include?

Your store buying rules, brand list, and pricing rubric, plus the photo intake workflow, AI analysis, POS-ready listing text, CSV export, and the manager approval workflow — configured to your shop during the pilot.

Will it work with my point of sale?

Tag to Rack exports a clean, mapped CSV designed to import into common resale POS systems. During setup we map the export to your fields, and we'll assist with integration on multi-location plans.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We're an early-stage partner and start with a short pilot. If it's making your intake desk faster and more consistent, you continue month to month or annually.

What about my data?

Your inventory data and photos are yours. See our Privacy & Data Policy for how information is handled, stored, and exported.
