Questions

Pricing & pilot FAQ

Does the AI make final buying decisions?

No. Tag to Rack only prepares recommendations. A manager approves every purchase, price, and listing before anything moves — that approval step is required and can't be turned off.

Why does it cost more than my POS software?

Tag to Rack isn't a point of sale — it's a sourcing layer that runs on top of the POS you already use. It's priced against the pre-screened inventory it brings in and the triage time it saves, not against POS subscriptions. Most stores find it costs less than adding part-time help, while keeping junk off the counter.

What does the setup include?

Your branded seller link and QR, store buying rules, brand list, and pricing rubric, plus AI pre-screening, automatic seller drop-off invites, POS-ready listing text, CSV export, and the manager approval workflow — configured to your shop during the pilot.

Will it work with my point of sale?

Tag to Rack exports a clean, mapped CSV designed to import into common resale POS systems. During setup we map the export to your fields, and we'll assist with integration on multi-location plans.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We're an early-stage partner and start with a short pilot. If it's filling your rack with pre-screened local inventory, you continue month to month or annually.

What about my data?

Your inventory data and photos are yours. See our Privacy & Data Policy for how information is handled, stored, and exported.
