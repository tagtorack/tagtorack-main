# Content — Tag to Rack (copy source of truth)

## Positioning
We help resale clothing stores intake, price, and list inventory faster with AI.
**Decision‑support, not automation** — the AI recommends, the manager approves.

## Hero
- **Headline:** AI intake, pricing, and listing support for resale clothing stores.
- **Sub:** Photograph the front, back, and tag. Tag to Rack drafts a structured intake
  card — brand, size, condition, pricing range, and POS‑ready listing text — in seconds.
  Your manager approves every call.
- **CTAs:** Book a pilot · See the demo

## Target customers
Consignment stores · thrift boutiques · children's resale · women's resale boutiques ·
outdoor gear resale · sneaker & streetwear resale · small multi‑location chains.

## The setup offer (AI clothing intake setup includes)
1. Store buying rule setup
2. Brand list setup
3. Pricing rubric
4. Photo intake workflow
5. AI item analysis
6. POS‑ready title + description
7. CSV export to POS
8. Weekly inventory + pricing reporting
9. Management approval workflow

## How it works (5 steps)
1. **Snap** — front / back / tag photos at the counter.
2. **Analyze** — AI drafts a structured intake card.
3. **Review** — staff check fields, flaws, and pricing.
4. **Approve** — manager confirms buy / pass / consign + price.
5. **Export** — POS‑ready title, description, and CSV.

## Trust pillar (front and center)
"Tag to Rack prepares recommendations. It never finalizes a purchase, price, or listing
on its own — a store manager approves every decision." Confidence scores, editable fields,
full audit trail.
