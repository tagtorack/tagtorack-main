Privacy — for sellers

What happens to your photos and info.

Last updated · May 30, 2026

Short version: when you submit an item, your photos, item details, and contact info go to one resale store — the one whose link you clicked. They use it to decide whether to invite you to drop the item off. We don't sell your data. You can ask us to delete it any time.

1. What we collect

  • Photos of your item — front, back, brand tag, and any flaw photos you upload. Camera metadata (location, device) is stripped from the photo before it's stored.
  • Item details you typed — the type, brand, size, asking price, condition, and any notes.
  • Contact info — your name and email (required), phone and zip (optional, only used if the store wants to confirm a drop-off time or send a text update).
  • Spam-prevention signals — a hash of your IP address (not the address itself), browser type, and a Cloudflare Turnstile challenge result. Used to prevent abuse, never shared.

2. Who sees it

The store you submitted to receives a copy of your submission — photos, item details, your name, and your email. They use this to decide whether to invite you in for a drop-off. They do not get your phone, zip, or IP info unless you'd otherwise give it to them.

Tag to Rack staff can see your submission in our internal queue if our AI flags it for human review. We use it only to help the store make a decision.

We do not share your data with any third party for marketing. We do not sell your data, ever.

3. How long we keep it

  • If the store approves your item and you book a drop-off — we keep your photos and submission for one year, then delete.
  • If the store passes on your item, or doesn't respond within 7 days — we keep your photos for 30 days, then delete.
  • If you ask us to delete sooner — we delete within 24 hours.

4. Marketing

We don't send you marketing emails unless you check the optional box on the contact step that says you want to hear about other items the store is looking for. Even then, you can unsubscribe with one click in any email.

5. Right to delete

You can ask us to delete your submission at any time. Two ways:

  • Reply to the confirmation email we sent you with the word "delete" — we'll take care of it.
  • Email submissions@tagtorack.com with your submission ID.

When you ask to delete, we remove your contact info and item details from our database within 24 hours, and your photos from our storage within 24 hours after that.

6. Cookies and storage

The submission page saves your progress in your phone or computer's local storage so you don't lose what you typed if you accidentally close the tab. That's it. We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't load any third-party tracker scripts.

Cloudflare Turnstile (the anti-spam check) may set a short-lived cookie to verify you're a person. It's not used for tracking — see Cloudflare's privacy policy.

7. Security

Your photos are uploaded directly to encrypted storage. We use industry-standard transport security (HTTPS) for every page. Only the staff at the store you submitted to, and Tag to Rack engineers with a need to know, can view your submission.

8. Children

This service isn't for people under 18. Don't submit on someone else's behalf if they're a minor.

9. Contact

Questions about this notice? Email submissions@tagtorack.com.

This page is a plain-language description of how we handle data for the Tag to Rack Submit feature. It's not legal advice. Some details may differ between merchants — check the store's own privacy policy if you have specific questions.
